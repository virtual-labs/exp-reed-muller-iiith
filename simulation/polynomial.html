Instructions
    Quick theory overview:
  • A polynomial is a linear combination of monomials. The degree of the polynomial is the maximum among the degrees of its constituent monomials.
  • To evaluate a polynomial at some position, we just replace all the variables with the corresponding values to do computation. The only caveat in our case is that computations are done modulo 2.

    Procedure:
  • Step 1: Enter the degree of polynomial \(P(\textbf{X})\) and click "Check" to verify your answer. If your answer is correct you will move on to the next section.
  • Step 2: For the same polynomial, evaluate it all values in \(\mathbb{F}_2^3\). We assume the variables are indexed as \((X_1, X_2, X_3)\).
  • Step 3: Click "Check" to verify your answer. You can also click "Previous" to go back to the previous question or "New Question" to get a new polynomial.
